Background

I have been programming games since 2009, I started in Scratch where I tried to remake games like 'Tron' and 'Call of Duty' in 2D and from there I was hooked. My whole academic life since then has been driven by my passion for games and I was lucky enough to do 'Computing' at GCSE and ALEVEL (at 16 and 18). From this I built up an early foundation of good coding practices and by the time I got to the University of Lincoln for my undergraduate degree in computer science, I had already programmed in Assembly, Python, C# and C++. During Univeristy I gained an in-depth knowledge of C#, C++ and PhysX as well as learning Haskell, Pure OPP, Matlab, SQLite, Javascript, Java, Android development (using android studio) and in my spare time, extensive knowledge of Unity development. In this portfolio you will find practical projects I did in and before university and personal projects on the side, including a Fantasy RTS game I worked on and my most recent project in Procedual Content Generation for Action-Adventure games.
