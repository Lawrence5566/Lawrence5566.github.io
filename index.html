Background

I have been programming games since 2009, I started in Scratch where I tried to remake games like 'Tron', 'Halo' and 'Call of duty' in 2D and from there I was hooked. My whole academic life since then has been driven by my passion for games and I was lucky enough to do 'Computing' at GCSE and ALEVEL (at 16 and 18). From this I built up an early foundation of good coding practices and by the time I got to the University of Lincoln for my undergraduate degree in computer science, I had already programmed in Assembly, Python, C# and C++. During Univeristy I gained an in-depth knowledge of C#, C++ and PhysX as well as learning Haskell, Pure OPP, Matlab, SQLite, Javascript, Java and Android development (using android studio). In my spare time, I taught myself extensive knowledge of Unity which I used for my projects during university.

Two years on from achieving my First honours and I'm working for TT games, creators of the Lego games. A dream to be sure - where I've gained vast experience of industry development, tools such as peforce and swarm reviewing, the true importance of testing, debugging and of communication between artists, animators and designers to code something fit for the job and to specification. It's been an amazing experience and that 13 year old kid playing around in scratch still has his sights, his passions, set for more.

In this portfolio you will find practical projects I did in and before university and personal projects on the side, including a Fantasy RTS game I worked on and my most recent project in Procedual Content Generation for Action-Adventure games.

Unfortunately due to NDA, I can't show what I've been working on in the last 2 years at TT games but to be sure I will show it off on release!
